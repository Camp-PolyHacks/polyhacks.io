FAQ

What is a Hackathon?

Camp PolyHacks is a weekend event where designers, developers, entrepreneurs can collaborate, learn, and create. The goal is to work within a team to ideate, design, pitch, and market a fully functioning product over a short period of time. It’s learn by doing at its best.

When is it happening?

This year’s Hackathon will be kicking off on Friday, January 18 at 5 PM. We’ll be hacking until the awards ceremonies the next day on Saturday, January 19 till 9 PM.

Where will it be?

Camp PolyHacks is taking place in Cal Poly’s brand new HotHouse, an incredible 13,000 square foot space in the heart of downtown San Luis Obispo.

How many people will be participating in this event?

In the past two years, we have had around 100-120 participants, and 20–30 company representatives.

How are you different from other hackathons?

Camp PolyHacks is a student-run event targeted on combining social entrepreneurship with technological development. We challenge our multidisciplinary campers to focus on solving pressing, civic-based problems presented by companies and community leaders.

What is the “Local Table”?

This year, we are want to continue involving the local community in our hackathon. The Local Table will showcase products and information on local goods, shops, and organizations.
