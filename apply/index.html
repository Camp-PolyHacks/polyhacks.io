Camp PolyHacks

Apply to join the Camp Polyhacks Team!

Camp PolyHacks is a student-run, multidisciplinary hackathon dedicated to social entrepreneurship and human-centered design. Started in 2016, we’ve helped over 600 Hackers (Developers), Hipsters (Designers), and Hustlers (Entrepreneurs) experience a learn by doing marathon of collaboration, learning, and creativity.

Why be a Trailblazer?

Experience

Gain practical experience and skills in a growth-mindset environment. A few skills you’ll learn and hone are: growth hacking, product/project management, fundraising, and graphic design.

Team

A close-knit team comprised of Cal Poly’s most passionate and brilliant students. Broaden and improve your soft skills - communication, attitude, work ethic. We may not have a 4.0 GPA, but are driven to building a better world.

Connections

Build relationships and network with students and industry professionals. Recruiters actively hire at the hackathon, so you’ll have priority when landing a job with top sponsors, such as Amazon and Microsoft.

Interested? Which team do you see yourself in?

Design & Tech Execute the creative direction of the event! You are the visual evangelist for all things Camp PolyHacks.
  • Design all collateral necessary for the event - signage, stickers, logo, assets.
  • Develop the event website, application systems, and all technological needs.
  • Cooperate with Marketing to administer and publish marketing graphics.
Sponsorship We wouldn’t have this event without your help! Initiate and establish strong relationships with companies.
  • Secure potential Sponsors by attending networking events, career fairs, company firm visits, etc and negotiating partnerships.
  • Strategically maintain email campaigns, manage our budget and event finances.
  • Build the sponsor mentorship program during and leading up to the event.
Marketing Share our hackathon with Cal Poly students and beyond! Update campers through social media, our blog, and more to get them ready and pumped for the event.
  • Collaborate with Design to solidify our content, promotion, and engagement strategy.
  • Identify untapped markets to increase awareness and demand across campus.
  • Leverage the tools necessary to manage and promote our social media.
Event & Experience Ensure the event is an incredible experience for everyone! You are responsible for making sure the exeprience meets everyone's expectations.
  • Conduct all aspects of event planning from conception to completion.
  • Plan the schedule of the event- organize caterers, workshops, activities, and more.
  • Create resources to prepare campers for the event such as what roles they can play in a multidisciplinary team, technical tutorials, and more.
