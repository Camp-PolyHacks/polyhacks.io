    What is Camp PolyHacks?

    Camp PolyHacks is a student-run, multidisciplinary hackathon focused on social entrepreneurship and human centered design.

    In 48 hours, students of all backgrounds compete to build businesses or any tech product to solve a problem currently impacting the community.

    Camp PolyHacks values...

    Problem Solving

    Camp PolyHacks is a Learn By Doing experience. We encourage campers of all levels to step outside of their comfort zone to learn new skills and tackle problems head-on.

    Multidisciplinary Teams

    This hackathon goes beyond coding. Designers, entrepreneurs, and developers work together in teams to unite skill sets, experiences, and perspectives to execute ideas — just as you would in the workforce.

    Social Entrepreneurship

    We believe in using technology for good. Campers are made aware of important problems in the community and Camp Polyhacks is a platform to solve them.

    FAQ

    What is a Hackathon?

    Camp PolyHacks is a weekend event where designers, developers, entrepreneurs can collaborate, learn, and create. The goal is to work within a team to ideate, design, pitch, and market a fully functioning product over a short period of time. It’s learn by doing at its best.

    When is it happening?

    This year’s Hackathon will be kicking off on Friday, January 18 at 5 PM. We’ll be hacking until the awards ceremonies the next day on Saturday, January 19 till 9 PM.

    How do I sign up for Camp PolyHacks?

    You can sign up for Camp PolyHacks through the simple application we will open in November. Stay tuned!

    How can I sponsor Camp PolyHacks?

    You can sponsor Camp PolyHacks by reaching out to us at team.camppolyhacks@gmail.com. All information is located in the Sponsorship section above or the packet here.

    When/Where is Camp PolyHacks?

    Camp PolyHacks will be held on January 18-19, 2019 at the Hot House, SLO’s entrepreneurship space in downtown right above Ross.

    What should I expect on the day of the event?

    The schedule will be released closer to the event, but you can expect unlimited free food, snacks, drinks, helpful company mentors/resources, and exciting activities to take a break from hacking.

    How much does it cost?

    Camp PolyHacks is 100% Free!

    What do I need to bring?

    Bring your laptop, chargers, sleeping bags/materials if you’re staying overnight, and your A-game!

    Do I need to be an experienced coder?

    Not at all! We welcome campers of all backgrounds and experience levels. This is your time to learn something new.

    What are the prizes and how are projects judged?

    Each team pitches and is judged based on their Business Model, Technical Capability, and User Experience. Past prizes include Google DayDreams and Amazon Echos.

    Will I be able to sleep during the hackathon?

    Yes, there will be places available for teams to rest at the Hot House overnight.

    How do I form teams and how big can they be?

    Teams can be up to 10 people and if you don’t have a team yet, there will be time on Friday to ﬁnd one.
